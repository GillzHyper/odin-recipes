Pasta

Pasta is one of the simplest dishes to prepare, yet it has so much variety. Using different sauces, vegetables, or even pasta shapes make a massive difference. This is espesscially important because you can easily substitute and change ingredients and be left with a new flavour without the hassle of learning a new recipe

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Seasoning
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Chop veggies and mix them into your pan along with ur tomatoe sauce
  2. Add pasta and seasoning to the pan and let it simmer, mix regularly(it should take around 30 minutes to cook)
  3. Enjoy

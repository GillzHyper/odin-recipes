Pizza

The best way to start master home cooking is repetetion while slowly experimenting. Luckily, one of the customizable meals is the beloved Pizza

In order to create a well made pizza, we need start off by prepping the ingredients. For a simple cheese pizza, we only need 3 ingredients:

Ingredients

  • dough(can be found at local bakeries)
  • pizza sauce(tomatoe paste will do fine)
  • cheese( I recommend buying the large slabs of cheese and grating them yourself)

    • Steps

Now that we have our ingredients, its time to start cooking by following these simple instructions:

  1. On a lightly floured surface(you can use your counter), stretch the dough into circle. slowly move it onto a non-stick baking sheet.
  2. Use a spoon to spread the pizza sauce onto the dough, make sure to leave the edges completly untouched to form that crust
  3. sprinkle your cheese over the pizza sauce (no need to be shy)
  4. put the sheet into the oven for roughly 10 minutes at 425 digrees, make sure to check it occasionally
  5. Enjoy
